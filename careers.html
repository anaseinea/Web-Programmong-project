Benefits

Family Health Finance
We strive to be a great place to work for people with families. Our programs make it easier and give you an extra hand to make sure your family feels cared for at every stage of life. We can help you even more. We’re committed to building a healthy community—one person at a time. But health is more than just getting the care you need. It’s about getting and staying healthy in all areas of your life. We offer saving and investment programs to help define and build on your financial goals and give you peace of mind as you plan for your future.
Housing Sick Leave Retirement
We offer housing for all employees (including interns!). Housing is provided in the form of campus. We have 2 campuses in the meantime and you will be assigned to one depending on your position in the company. Employees are allowed to take 30 day leave each year. It is required though to give prior notice before leaving more than 2 weeks ahead. The leave is fully paid. You also can request extra days but won't be paid. Defined benefit plan guarantees a certain payout at retirement, according to a fixed formula which usually depends on the member's salary and the years.
