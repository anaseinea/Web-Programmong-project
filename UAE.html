  • Hyperloop Project in Dubai

    Live faster

Hyperloop One, the high-speed transport system set to revolutionise transportation in the UAE, has released a video teasing Dubai with a November 8, 2016 announcement.

The team behind Hyperloop One released a You Tube video on Sunday, and in the promotional teaser, said:

“Dubai to Abu Dhabi in 12 minutes, Dubai to Riyadh in 48 minutes, Dubai to Doha in 23 minutes, Dubai to Muscat in 27 minutes.”

Once completed, the high-speed transport system will revolutionize the way people travel, allowing UAE passengers to travel hundreds of kilometres in a matter of minutes through air-cushioned pods speeding through a semi-vacuumed tube.
